Returns and Exchanges Policy

  • - Return packages must be postmarked within 30 days of the order delivering to you. Returns postmarked after this time frame will be subject to denial.
  • - Returns postmarked more than 10 days after the delivery date will have a $7.99 shipping fee as well as a 20% restocking fee per item deducted from the refund amount. If you choose to use your own shipping label, only the itemized restocking fee will be deducted from your refund amount.
  • - No returns, refunds, or online credits are available for final sale items of any price.
  • - Returns must be received Unwashed, Unworn, and in original condition.
  • - Items must be returned with all original tags attached.
  • - Items must be returned with protective packaging, such as the polybag or box it was originally shipped with, to keep them safe in transit back to us.
  • - Beauty & Wellness products must be returned unused, in their original packaging, and with the seal unbroken.
  • - Items sold as sets must be returned with the correct sizes, as sold.
  • - Items returned with apparent use, makeup, hairs or fiber, dust or dirt, deodorant, perfume, or otherwise, do not meet our return policy mentioned above and will be denied.
  • - Gift cards cannot be returned or refunded.

FAQ's

Shipping

Do you ship P.O. Boxes?

No

I wanted to add an item to my order, can I make a change before it ships?

Once an order is placed, it goes straight to our warehouse department to process and ship. To ensure it gets to you as soon as we can, unfortunately, we CAN NOT make changes to the order placed.

Can I exchange my order?

Yes, you have 30 days after purchase to request and make an exchange. Please contact us at hello@KDCactive.com

Why is my order not delivered at the estimated time?

Shipping estimates are provided and fulfilled by UPS. We have been advised to let our customers know that this can be affected by Covid-19, weather, etc. We apologize ahead of time if you do not receive your order in a timely manner.

Product and Inventory

Are your garments True to Size?

In short, yes. They have spandex in them for a little give and comfort, of course.

What if I’m between sizes?

Our suggestion would be to size down for a more compressive fit and size up for a more relaxed fit.

The color, size, or style I want is sold out. Will there be a restock?

Generally, our classic styles will be restocked but this isn’t the case for all styles. Please check back in with us periodically.

Refunds

When will my refund process?

Refunds should be processed in 10 to 12 days. Please check back in with us if it exceeds that time.
